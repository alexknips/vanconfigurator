Do you want to build every single item in a van conversion yourself?

Maybe, but more likely you want to spend time to enjoy your van and use it on weekends or fulltime. The van conversion market is big and the van configuration tool will help you get the products necessary for your van conversion.

Selection of features

The van configurator will help the customer choosing products fitting their needs while etimating the costs, necessary time and skill.

Configuration

After selection of the wanted features the user selects matching products to their liking.

Why?

Van conversions are hard. I have gone through the process and have seem many skilled people struggle and this tools aims to help people achieve their dream van.

  • Custom conversions are expensive

    Luxury custom conversion vans are out of reach for many customers often costing 100k USD plus the cost of the van.

  • Quality

    We feature quality products only and work together with awesome vendors.

  • What to buy?

    The tool helps you get a good grasp of what is achievable in your budget and skillset.

  • What layout fits me?

    Let's figure out what layout fits you and what products fit that layout.

  • Why not just build it myself?

    Are you a carpenter or have you build more than 5 pieces of furniture before?

  • Why not build it out of 2by2 or 4by4?

    Sqeaking, constant vibration, weight to name a few issues.
